Let's Roll

You control a ball. Collect the crystal in time to unlock the next platform.

Inspired by Unity's official "Roll a ball" tutorial, I wanted to see what a more advanced version of the game would look like. I used photoshop to make some good textures and programmed several steps to randomly generate around the platform conveniently. Once the player reaches the crystal, the timer resets and the player is ready to climb their way to the next platform.
